Take language learning to the next level with VILLA VR.


VILLA (Virtual Immersive Language Learning Application) VR is a virtual reality language learning RPG. Users will be swept into a fantasy world where they will be able to practice speaking in their language of choice. For those who are new to the language or may need a refresher on some key phrases/vocabulary, there will be a small assistant to guide them around the world if they run into language speaking hurdles. Our goal is to give our learner a seamless language immersion experience as well as a judgment free place to practice speaking in their desired language. Our learners will also dive into a mountain of quests and events, where they learn while exploring this world. Welcome to VILLA VR!

About Us

Our team is passionate about incorportating extended reality (XR) into our everyday lives and see a lot of potential in its impact in making education more accessible. We see hands-on learning in mixed reality as being accessible from a few button presses and hope to build upon this idea in our mission to develop XR applications that educate.
